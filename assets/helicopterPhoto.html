About Us


Northwest Dynamics, Inc. (NDI) is a design, development, and manufacturing company.  

AS9100D, FAA Repair Station, and EASA Certified.
​
NDI specializes in hydro-mechanical actuators, fluidic control valves, rotor brakes, and directional control valves. 

NDI has been in business for over 30 years and is located in Vancouver, WA.
